# Life Expectancy Analysis

Project 1 dives into the WHO life expectancy dataset to understand global health indicators.

- Loaded and cleaned the data, stripping column whitespace and standardizing country names, then mapped ISO-3 country codes.
- Audited missingness, addressed inconsistencies, and prepared features for downstream analysis.
- Explored relationships among socioeconomic, health, and vaccination metrics with plots and nullity checks.
- Applied clustering (k-means) and PCA to study country groupings and reduce dimensionality for visualization.
